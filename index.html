My Work

TokyoShark - Online Shopping App

TokyoShark is an app for people who want to buy Japanese goods only found at conventions. On this mobile app, users can buy plush/figures/accessories/scale models/graphic novels/video games. It can be used anytime, even when conventions are not going on, and so users can get their nerd on at anytime of the day or year.

Three Thousand G's

Three Thousand G's is designed to help find you the best deal on an item, no matter where it's sold. All you have to do is search or scan your time, then sit back and check out where the best price is - and links you right to it! Waiting for an item to go on sale? No problem! Three Thousand G's also has a price tracker function that will alert you when your item drops below the set price threshold. Save your money with us!
