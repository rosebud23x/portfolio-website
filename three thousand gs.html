Three Thousand Gs

Three Thousand Gs is an app for people who are looking for the best price across multiple websites. On this mobile app, users can search any item to show multiple price results from any store and choose the lowest price.

Problem

There are a multitude of apps on the market today that to help shoppers find the best deals, they range from simply showing coupons for certain products to housing flyers from department stores. Most only help the user with one aspect of getting the best deal, users need an all in one app that is easy to use.

Solution

This is app designed to create an easy to navigate design for users of every age needing to find a deal. Icons should be easy to recognize with distinctive colors to differentiate feathers and buttons.
