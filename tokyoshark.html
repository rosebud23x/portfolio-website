TokyoShark

TokyoShark is an app for people who want to buy Japanese goods only found at conventions. On this mobile app, users can buy plush/figures/accessories/scale models/graphic novels/video games. It can be used anytime, even when conventions are not going on, and so users can get their nerd on at anytime of the day or year.

Problem

The best place to purchase Japanese goods is to visit anime/comic conventions, but not every user has the ability to do so. For users who can't afford to attend or travel to time specific conventions, it's hard to find a reliable online shop that sells genuine goods.

Solution

To create an online shop that comes from a team of convention staff who know the ins and outs of reliable vendors. Users are able to purchase convention quality items any time off the day or year, where at home or on the go.

Logo

Our logo represents our love for Japan and all it holds. Our design represents the hunt for merch in any treacherous waters. We want to portray a fun, and playful look that invites anyone with the sound of J-Pop in their heart to join. There is also a secondary style that is simpler and can come in a variety of colors based on need.
