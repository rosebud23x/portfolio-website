TokyoShark

TokyoShark is an app for people who want to buy Japanese goods only found at conventions. On this mobile app, users can buy plush/figures/accessories/scale models/graphic novels/video games. It can be used anytime, even when conventions are not going on, and so users can get their nerd on at anytime of the day or year.
